Memorice mode

This is the classic version of the game, where you must pair the description cards to their drawing, but in the fashion of a memorice game. Let's go!

Set up

 Designate a judge, the rest of the players will form 2 to 3 teams (min. 1 player/team).
 Shuffle all playing cards and lay them face down in rows on a flat surface.

How to play

 The starting team flips over one image and one description card.
 The description must be read aloud and the image visible to all players.
 As a team, decide whether or not the description card matches the image card. Discussion is encouraged.
 If a match is declared, the judge will use the solutions to make a ruling (see Scoring).
 Once scoring is complete, the next team takes their turn.
 The game continues until all cards are paired.

Scoring

 If a match is declared:
         If correct, the team takes the pair and earns 1 point.
         If incorrect, the team replaces cards face down and loses 1 point.
 If no match is declared:
        Cards are replaced and no points are lost or earned.

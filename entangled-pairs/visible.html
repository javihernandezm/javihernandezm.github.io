Visible mode

This is the an easier version of the game, where you must pair the description cards to their drawing all at the same time, while all cards are visible. Let's go!

Set up

 Designate a judge, the rest of the players will form one team.
 Shuffle all playing cards and lay them face up in rows on a flat surface, separating the description and image cards.

How to play

 Choose any description card and read it aloud.
 As a team, choose a drawing to match the description in the card. Discussion is encouraged.
 Take the pair of cards and put them aside, with the drawing and the title of the card visible.
 The game continues until all cards are paired.
 For every entangled pair correctly matched, the team earns 1 point.

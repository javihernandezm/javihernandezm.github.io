Rincón Matemático

A YouTube that me and some friends from university created to teach olympic-level mathematics to children in Chile.

This channel was an initiative from me and some friends from university, all who were former participants of mathematics olympiads during high school, where we met each other. The project was sponsored by the National Mathematics Championship in Chile.

For all of us, mathematics was a sport, and as such, one needs training in order to become good at it. For this reason, we wanted to help students who had talent but did not have the resources to receive formal training in mathematics, so we created this YouTube channel to solve exercises and teach techniques useful in mathematical thinking and particularly for olympiads.

The videos are available in the channel Rincón Matemático (content in Spanish).
